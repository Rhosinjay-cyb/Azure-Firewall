Directions to COSTECH (23 Folawewo Street, Allen Avenue, Onigbongbo, Ikeja, Lagos)


For People Driving:


From Ikeja Under Bridge:



Head north along Awolowo Way towards Allen Avenue.

At the Allen Roundabout, take the exit on your right onto Allen Avenue.

Continue straight on Allen Avenue for approximately 1000 meters.

Make a U-turn at the end of the road(As if going back to Allen Roundabout).

Look out for Providus Bank on your right as a landmark.

Immediately after Providus Bank, take a right turn onto Folawewo Street.

Drive straight till you come to a T-Junction and then trun right.

You will arrive at a black estate gate.

Tell the security personnel you are going to COSTECH Computers and you will be directed accordignly. 



From Third Mainland Bridge:



Drive along the bridge towards the mainland and exit at Oworonshoki.

Take the exit onto Gbagada Expressway and continue towards the Oregun/Alausa exit.

Merge onto Oregun Road and head towards Alausa Secretariat.

Turn left onto Kudirat Abiola Way and follow it until you reach the Allen Roundabout.

At the roundabout, take the first exit onto Allen Avenue.

Continue straight on Allen Avenue for approximately 1000 meters.

Make a U-turn at the end of the road(As if going back to Allen Roundabout).

Look out for Providus Bank on your right as a landmark.

Immediately after Providus Bank, take a right turn onto Folawewo Street.

Drive straight till you come to a T-Junction and then trun right.

You will arrive at a black estate gate.

Tell the security personnel you are going to COSTECH Computers and you will be directed accordignly. 



For People Using Public Transport:


From Ikeja Under Bridge:



From Ikeja Under Bridge, take a tricycle(keke) heading to "Allen-Inside"

Get off at Hospital Bus-stop.

Cross to the other side of the road.

Walk straight down into the street at the other side of the road.

Turn right at the T-Junction at the end of the road.

You will arrive at a black estate gate.

Tell the security personnel you are going to COSTECH Computers and you will be directed accordignly. 



From Oshodi:



Take a bus or a shared cab to Ikeja Under Bridge.

From Ikeja Under Bridge, Follow the directions above till you get to the loacation.



Landmarks to Note:



Allen Roundabout

Zenith Bank on Allen Avenue opposite the road just before Folawewo Street.

Providus Bank on Allen Avenue close to Folawewo Street.
